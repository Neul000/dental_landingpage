About Us

DR IZZI DENTAL CLINIC, also known as Klinik Pergigian Dr Izzi, was established in Pandan Indah, Kuala Lumpur in April 2015 strategically between Pantai Hospital Ampang and Menara MPAJ. Since then we strive to grow, by providing quality holistic dentistry in an upscale environment with the latest technology and at affordable pricing. We just started operating our second branch in Seksyen 13, Shah Alam beside Hospital Umra the end of April 2018 and planning to open more branches to provide the same hospitality to an area near you.
